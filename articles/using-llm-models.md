# Using LLM Models in 5 Points

20 April 2026

LLM models are useful when you treat them like smart assistants, not magic machines.

1. Be clear with your prompt. The better your instruction, the better the output.

2. Use them for drafting, summarizing, brainstorming, and coding help, but always review the result.

3. Give context. A model works better when it knows your goal, audience, and constraints.

4. Do not trust every answer blindly. LLMs can sound confident even when they are wrong.

5. Combine human judgment with AI speed. The best results usually come from collaboration, not full automation.
